First choose the calendar month you want to compare (must match the Nokia grid). The server uses Tesseract OCR on screenshots when no CSV is given. Results are compared to this app’s leave tracker (pending and approved leave for every roster member, same rules as the dashboard grid). Non-empty Nokia day cells (except -, ., 0, etc.) count as leave in Nokia.
